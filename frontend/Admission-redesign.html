What You Need

Required Documents Checklist

Prepare these documents for a smooth application process

Required

Birth Certificate

Original or certified copy of the child's birth certificate issued by the relevant authority

  • Original or certified copy
  • Clear and legible
  • PDF or JPG format
Optional

Previous School Report

Latest academic report card from your child's previous school (if applicable)

  • Most recent term
  • Official school stamp
  • All pages included
Required

Passport Photographs

Two recent passport-size color photographs of the child with white background

  • Passport size (35x45mm)
  • White background
  • Recent (within 6 months)
Required

Immunization Records

Up-to-date immunization card showing all required vaccinations and medical history

  • Complete vaccination record
  • Signed by medical officer
  • Current health status
Required

Parent/Guardian ID

Valid identification document of parent or legal guardian (National ID or Passport)

  • National ID or Passport
  • Valid and not expired
  • Both sides if ID card
Optional

Transfer Letter

Official transfer letter from previous school on school letterhead (if transferring)

  • School letterhead
  • Signed by head teacher
  • Official school stamp
