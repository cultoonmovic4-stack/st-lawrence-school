FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the school's operating hours?

The school operates Monday to Friday from 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM. Our office is open from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekdays and 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM on Saturdays.

How can I schedule a school tour?

You can schedule a school tour by calling us at +256 772 420 506 or sending an email to stlawrencejuniorschoolkabowa@gmail.com. Tours are available on weekdays by appointment.

What is the admission process?

The admission process involves filling out an application form, submitting required documents, attending an interview, and completing the registration process. Visit our Admission page for detailed information.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we offer school bus transportation services to various routes around Kampala. Please contact our office for route details and transportation fees.

What is the teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain a 1:15 teacher-to-student ratio to ensure personalized attention and quality education for every child.

Are meals provided for students?

Yes, we provide nutritious meals for all students. Boarding students receive three meals daily, while day scholars can opt for lunch services.
