About St. Lawrence Junior School

St. Lawrence Junior School Kabowa is a premier mixed day and boarding primary school that combines academic excellence with holistic development. Established in 2010, we offer both nursery and primary education in a nurturing environment.

We maintain our commitment to fostering an inclusive learning environment where every child can thrive and reach their full potential. Our dedicated faculty and modern facilities ensure that students receive the best possible education while developing strong character and life skills.
