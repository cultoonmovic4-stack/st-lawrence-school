Our Approach

World-Class Education

At St. Lawrence Junior School, we provide a comprehensive and balanced curriculum that combines academic rigor with character development. Our teaching methodology focuses on developing critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills that prepare students for success in the 21st century.

We follow the Uganda National Curriculum with enhanced programs in Mathematics, Science, Languages, and the Arts. Our experienced teachers use modern teaching methods and technology to make learning engaging and effective.

How We Teach

Our Teaching Philosophy

Student-Centered Learning

Every child is unique. We place students at the heart of learning, encouraging active participation, questions, and exploration. Small class sizes ensure personalized attention.

Hands-On Learning

Learning by doing makes concepts stick. Students engage in experiments, projects, and practical activities that transform abstract ideas into concrete understanding.

Technology-Enhanced Education

Modern technology amplifies learning. Interactive whiteboards, educational software, computer labs, and digital resources prepare students for tomorrow's world while making today's lessons more engaging and effective.

Continuous Assessment & Growth

We believe in ongoing evaluation and feedback. Regular assessments help us track progress, identify strengths, and address challenges early.

Our comprehensive assessment system includes:

  • ✓ Weekly progress monitoring
  • ✓ Monthly parent-teacher conferences
  • ✓ Termly detailed report cards
  • ✓ Mock examinations for PLE preparation
  • ✓ Individual learning plans for every student
