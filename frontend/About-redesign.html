Our Story

Building Futures Through Quality Education

The Beginning - 2010

Founded in 2010, St. Lawrence Junior School Kabowa has grown from a small community school into one of the most respected primary educational institutions in the region. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide quality education that transforms lives and builds character.

Growth & Excellence

Over the years, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence while nurturing the holistic development of every child. Our dedicated team of educators, modern facilities, and comprehensive curriculum have helped hundreds of students achieve their dreams and excel in their academic pursuits.

Today & Beyond

Today, St. Lawrence Junior School stands as a beacon of educational excellence, combining traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Journey

Our History of Excellence

A remarkable journey of growth, achievement, and unwavering commitment to educational excellence

2010

The Foundation

St. Lawrence Junior School Kabowa was established with a vision to provide quality education to the community. Starting with just 50 students and 5 dedicated teachers, we laid the foundation for what would become a beacon of educational excellence.

50 Students
5 Teachers
Est. 2010
2013
2013

Major Expansion

We expanded our facilities with new classrooms, a modern library, and science laboratories. Student enrollment grew to 200, and we introduced boarding facilities to accommodate students from distant areas.

200 Students
Boarding Added
2016

Academic Excellence

Achieved 100% PLE pass rate for the first time with 80% scoring Division 1. Won multiple inter-school competitions in academics, sports, and arts. Recognized as one of the top primary schools in the region.

100% Pass Rate
Regional Champions
2016
2019
2019

Digital Transformation

Launched state-of-the-art ICT laboratory with 50 computers. Introduced digital learning platforms and smart classrooms. Became one of the first primary schools in the area to integrate technology into daily learning.

Smart Classes
